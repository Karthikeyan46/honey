Frequently Asked Questions

Does your honey expire? +
Pure, raw honey never truly expires. It can crystallize over time, which is a sign of purity. Simply place the jar in warm water to reliquefy it.
Is this honey raw and unfiltered? +
Yes, all our honey is 100% raw, unfiltered, and unheated to preserve its natural enzymes and nutritional benefits.
How should I store my honey? +
Store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. There is no need to refrigerate pure honey.
Do you ship across India? +
Yes! We deliver pan-India. Orders are carefully packed to prevent leakage and typically reach you within 3–5 business days. WhatsApp us for delivery timelines to your pin code.
What is the difference between Kombu and Wild Forest Honey? +
Kombu honey (stingless bee honey) is rarer, slightly tangy, and has a higher medicinal value with powerful antioxidants. Wild Forest Honey is harvested from large Apis bees in the deep forest, offering a robust, multi-floral flavour. Both are raw and unprocessed.
Can diabetics consume your honey? +
While raw honey has a lower glycemic index than refined sugar, we recommend consulting your doctor before consuming honey if you are diabetic. Kombu honey is often preferred by those monitoring blood sugar levels.
